Live demo of the guest-facing mobile form, shown in a handset view for sales walkthroughs.

Why buyers care about this

The form is where the experience starts. If this part feels fast and easy, the whole activation works better.

Less friction

Guests complete the flow themselves, which keeps queues moving and reduces staff intervention.

Cleaner handover

The QR links guest input directly to production, so operators can work faster with fewer errors.

Better perceived value

The experience feels considered from the first tap, which lifts the perceived value of the finished item.

How it works

The same four-step flow is used on the day: simple for guests, structured for operators.

1

Enter details

The guest fills in a short form on their phone.

2

Review and confirm

They check the details before submitting.

3

Receive the QR

They receive a QR code for the engraving handoff.

4

Engrave and hand over

The team scans it, engraves the item and hands it over.
